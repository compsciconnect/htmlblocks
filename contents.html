Free. Accessible. Student Centered.

The Maryland Center for Women in Computing Curriculum In A Box project is geared toward middle and high school aged students and their parents and teachers.

Our goal is to encourage students of all genders, abilities, and races to learn about computer science and about computer scientists.

Our resources are accessible to those with disabilities through screen reader compatible pages and video closed captioning.

The content presented here has been used successfully for summer and after school programs at the University of Maryland and in Prince Georges County Public Schools.
